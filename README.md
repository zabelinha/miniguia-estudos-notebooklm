DESCRIPTION
This notebook contains information about AI careers, showing how AI is shaping the job market and changing the skills required. It was created to guide anyone through the scaring feeling of choosing an career that can turn obsolete in the next years, and to help them understand how to become a professional in this area.
In the first instance, it was meant to answer only technical questions about my career choice within AI engineering, however, it became a way to explore alternatives for a better professional development, even in a scenario of uncertainty. That’s why I trained it to “put some heart into it” in a scenario of fear and insecurity.

CHALLENGES FACED DURING THE PROJECT
First, I tried to tell it how to behave when facing an emotional situation, writing it as a source. However, I didn’t get the best response. So, I rewrote the command as a fact rather than an instruction. Now, instead of saying “It is documented that feeling insecure is normal”, it affirms: “Don’t worry, feeling insecure is normal".
